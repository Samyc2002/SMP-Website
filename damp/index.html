Introduction

The Department Academic Mentorship Program (DAMP) aims to create a support system for students facing academic as well as personal difficulties. The DAMP also provides senior mentors to the sophomores entering the CSE, EE and ME depatment. The DAMP Team aims to provide easy access to information regarding courses offered by the departments, research projects being undertaken, internships and the like.
